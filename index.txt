Project #3:

	Name: Voodoo Robotic Arm

	Purpose: To be able to control a custom hand made robotic arm with a mini "voodoo doll" type 

	Components: Arduino Uno, USB cord to connect Arduino to PC, stepper motor+driver, 2 potentiometers, pressure film sensor, 5v external battery power source, servo motor driver, 4 digital servo motors.

	- Libraries used for source code in "IDE Libraries" folder
 
CAD Platform: Onshape.com (free)
